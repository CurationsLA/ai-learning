About CurationsLA

Hyperlocal LA media. Human-curated. AI-supported. Good vibes only.

What Is CurationsLA?

CurationsLA is a hyperlocal Los Angeles media platform that tells the stories of LA's neighborhoods, people, and culture.

We're not another news site. We're not clickbait. We're not algorithmic noise.

We're LA, telling LA's story.

Curated by humans. Supported by AI. Free and ungated. Non-political. Community-first.

Why CurationsLA Exists

The Problem

Local media is dying. The outlets that remain are:
- Paywalled (inaccessible)
- Clickbait-driven (low quality)
- Corporate-owned (disconnected from community)
- Algorithm-optimized (designed for engagement, not truth)

LA deserves better.

The Solution

Human-curated local storytelling, amplified by AI.

CurationsLA is:
- Free ‚Äì No paywall, no subscription, no gatekeeping
- Human-curated ‚Äì Real people finding and sharing real stories
- AI-supported ‚Äì Technology helps us scale without losing quality
- Community-focused ‚Äì We tell the stories that matter to Angelenos
- Good vibes only ‚Äì No politics, no rage-bait, no division
- Trusted ‚Äì Transparent about sources, methods, and AI use

How It Works

The Curation Model

1. Human Discovery
Our curators (real LA humans) find stories worth sharing:
- Local events and happenings
- Community initiatives
- Cultural moments
- Neighborhood gems
- People doing interesting things

2. AI Support
AI helps us:
- Research and verify information
- Draft initial story frameworks
- Optimize for clarity and accessibility
- Manage publication workflows
- Surface patterns and connections

3. Human Final Say
Every story is:
- Reviewed by human curators
- Fact-checked and verified
- Edited for voice and tone
- Published with full transparency

Human judgment. AI capability. Community trust.
